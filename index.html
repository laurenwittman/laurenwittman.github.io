About Lauren

Lauren Wittman is a global real estate advisor with Premier Sotheby’s International Realty, serving the beautiful Naples area. Originally from the North Chicago suburbs, she brings a blend of Midwest warmth and coastal cool to everything she does.

Lauren graduated from Drake University in Iowa with degrees in environmental science, writing, and psychology—a unique mix that helps her think creatively, understand people on a deeper level, and communicate clearly, both in and out of the real estate world. Florida isn’t just where she lives—it’s what inspires her.

Clients love Lauren for her honest, down-to-earth approach and her ability to make the whole buying or selling process feel seamless (and even fun). Known for fostering win-win outcomes, Lauren believes that luxury real estate is not solely about exceptional properties but about curating exceptional experiences. She redefines luxury with authenticity, dedication, and an innate understanding of lifestyle needs.

Community involvement is key for Lauren, from supporting local causes to hosting pet-friendly events. Her dog Magnolia, featured over a dozen times on Chewy’s website, reflects her patient and polished style. When not working, you’ll find her paddle boarding, doing yoga, or grabbing a latte at a Naples coffee shop.
